Sheryians | DIT University, Dehradun

DIT University, Dehradun, India
Two days workshop | HTML / CSS / JS

A two days HTML/CSS/JS workshop held at DIT University on 22nd and 23rd of January 2018

This workshop was done under the Research Club of DIT University and schedule was divided into two days, for the first day, we started with HTML, explaining what HTML is and why it's important to learn it and then digging into more advanced concepts, later moved to CSS part and tried to understand why do we need CSS and how to apply CSS, then we finished first day with a project which was using FLEX-BOX. Second day started with ins and outs of JavaScript, and later moving to more advanced concepts and finally creating our second day project, a bubble game !
